Group Review - Group F

The group where you expect Portugal to top it but you’ve got Roberto Martinez at the helm so we don’t know what will happen. Let’s see.

Portugal

Really high favorites to go through as group toppers. They’ve got a really really good team together looking at it all. It’s the 6th Euros that Cristiano Ronaldo is a part of. Let’s see if he can bring the same Saudi form to this tournament.

Czech Republic

The only thing I remember from these lot was that unreal goal from Patrick Schick in the last tournament. They have a decent enough team with the likes of Schick, Hlozek, Soucek and so on who can make a difference but I think they could only go to 3rd maybe.

Turkey

These are the ones I think I’m backing to finish as group runner-up. They have a very very capable attack with the likes of Akturkoglu, Arda Guler and also Yildiz which is a blend of both youth and experience. You’ve obviously got Bayindir between the sticks. and the defense is alright except the full backs mostly and also the likes of Calhanoglu and Kokcu in midfield as well. They could be really good and I’m excited to see how they perform

Georgia

This is the first time they’re here in this prestigious tournament and they have some good talent with the likes of Mamardashvili between the sticks and also Kvaratskhelia who’s been really good since he burst onto the scene for Napoli. Maybe this is a change for the better and we’ll see more talent come through from this nation eventually.

My picks 1-4

  1. Portugal
  2. Turkey
  3. Czech Republic
  4. Georgia
