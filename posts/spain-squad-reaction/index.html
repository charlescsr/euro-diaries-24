Spain Squad Reaction

Alright. Got some good talent here and experience too. They’re in a group with Italy and Croatia, so it’s going to be tough. Let’s go through each of them for this 4-3-3 formation that they’re obviously going to go with

Goalkeeper

I think this is a nailed on pick. David Raya. Fantastic debut season with the Golden Glove with Arsenal in the Premier League. The quality of the others are good as well with Unai Simon and Alex Remiro, but Raya is definitely a shoo in.

Defense

At the time of writing this, we already know who’s got the axe, and it’s slightly surprising. But I think in terms of defenders you got Carvajal for RB who recently just won the Champions League with Real Madrid and a CB pairing of mostly Le Normand and Vivian, but Laporte could also be in there for balance on the left side. I think the LB is a lock in by Grimaldo because of the storming season he just had Bayer Leverkusen.

Midfield

We got 2 La Masia graduates from here in Pedri and Fermin after having good seasons for FC Barcelona, but I think only the former will start out of the 2 because you can’t look past Rodri for the CDM slot. The other midfield slot could be Merino for his physicality, and he’s been in good form for Real Sociedad.

Attack

To this day, I still question what possessed this manager to pick Alvaro freaking Morata as his captain, but since he is selected as that, he’s the lock in at the ST position. For backup, you got another Champions League winner in Joselu(Still unreal the world I’m in where someone who used to play for STOKE has just won the Champions League). The wingers have good options with the likes of Mikel Oyarzabal, Ferran Torres, Nico Williams and Lamine Yamal(The latter 2 being ones to watch). Ayoze Perez is also present there as an option after he’s hit really good form for Real Betis over this season as well.
