Group Review - Group B

A really hard group and you got to feel for Albania. But honestly you got quality everywhere in the other teams in Italy, Spain and Croatia. Let’s get into each team

Albania

I don’t think I know anybody there apart from Armando Broja who’s an on loan Chelsea ST at Fulham. He was honestly not that great and now he’s in the middle of a sale saga because of FFP. That’s a whole story for another time. I think I don’t see them come out of this group mostly because of the quality the other teams have.

Croatia

Still headed by the likes of Brozovic, Modric in yet another tournament after their 3rd place in the World Cup 2 years back. They still have a good core in defense with the likes of Gvardiol who’s gone to another level towards the end of the season for Man City, Juranovic who’s been good for them when they’ve played and Stanisic who was part of that dominant Leverkusen side. In midfield you’ve got Modric and Brozovic like I said. In attack is where I’ve always had a problem for them and looking at their squad it still doesn’t inspire confidence and they will look to Modric to deliver again. I know he will but they NEED a talisman badly

Spain

Their off field issues with the women’s side aside(I am obviously really against what that scumbag did and I’m glad he’s suspended and axed), this is a new look side that is somehow still rocking Jesus Navas at the very ripe age of 38. Carvajal is a serial winner I know that but he’s getting old too. But I think their defense aging aside, their midfield and attack quality could see them through but with where I’m predicting it maybe they could go through as the best 3rd placed team because I think Croatia have that little bit of determination to pull through and get that 2nd spot.

Italy

Them being the defending champions feels so weird to say given they DIDN’T play a damn World Cup 2 years back when they were expected to. But now with a change of manager and with major players being in form I’m backing them as the group winners and with a disciplinarian like Spalletti(He’s given some real strict rules for the players this time). Let’s see how they do because I’ve never seen a Spalletti system in play before

My picks 1-4

  1. Italy
  2. Croatia
  3. Spain - Might squeeze in as best 3rd placed team
